College Tuition-Salary Explorer

This webisite provides insight into the profitability of attending certain colleges in the United States. Colleges can be compared amongst all other schools, schools in their state, and schools of their size. Prospective students can also view tuitions depending on their residency, as some public universities have in-state pricing. In order to explore this viz, dropdowns can be selected to view specific filters, and a search bar can be used to find specific schools. In order to view a schools details, mouse over its icon or bar to temporarily view, or click on it to directly compare it to other schools. Press esc to return to the initial view from the search bar, and navigate to 'all' on the dropdowns to do the same.
